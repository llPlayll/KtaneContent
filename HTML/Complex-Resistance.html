On the Subject of Complex-Resistance

I'm so lost...

  • Initially, the module will show a complex number. Note down this number and press the status light.
  • Your friend lost themselves. Help them by guiding them through.
  • Convert the number into base-(1+i). The digits are:
    • 0, noted as 0 and could be inputted via center button;
    • +1, noted as + and could be inputted via right button;
    • -1, noted as - and could be inputted via left button;
    • +i, noted as i and could be inputted via up button;
    • -i, noted as ! and could be inputted via down button.
  • Input will be from least to most significant digits.
  • Red button undoes last input.
  • Green button submits the input.
