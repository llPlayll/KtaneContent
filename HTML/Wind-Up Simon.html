⚠️ IMPORTANT ⚠️
This is a module in the Wind-Up series.
Refer to Appendix WIND-UP KEY for important notes.

  • The module stores 4 stages of Simon Says, out of order, and only the last color.
  • You can turn the center key to view each stage’s color, flashed by the outer ring.*
  • Current viewed stage is indicated by one of 4 colored brackets lighting up. Stored order is Blue -> Yellow -> Green -> Red.*
  • Using the 4 given stages, submit a sequence of 4 colors. Change the viewed stage to reset your submission.
